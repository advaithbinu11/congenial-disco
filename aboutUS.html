Our Vision

To become the go-to digital hub for discovering and celebrating Austin's dynamic food truck scene, fostering a thriving local food economy, and ensuring seamless interactions between food truck owners, customers, and the city.

Our Mission

To create an accessible web and mobile platform that empowers food lovers to effortlessly discover and enjoy Austin's vibrant food truck scene. Our solution ensures a secure and user-friendly experience for both the City of Austin and food truck owners, facilitating real-time updates on locations, menus, and reviews. By bridging the gap between the community and local food vendors, we aim to celebrate and sustain the diverse culinary culture of Austin.
